How I improved a landing page

Mar 2025 • WebEditz

1️⃣ Spacing Good spacing makes a website clean, readable, and comfortable for users. Proper use of padding, margins, and white space helps separate sections, avoid clutter, and guide the user’s eyes smoothly through the page. --- 2️⃣ Contrast Contrast ensures that text and elements stand out clearly. Using strong color differences between background and text improves visibility, accessibility, and overall design quality. Good contrast helps users quickly understand the most important content. --- 3️⃣ CTA Placement CTA (Call-To-Action) buttons should be placed where users can easily notice and click them. Effective CTA placement includes putting buttons near important content, above the fold, and at the end of meaningful sections.
